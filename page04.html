Modern Tetris Versions

Puyo Puyo Tetris

An exciting crossover experience between two puzzle game giants. Puyo Puyo Tetris combines the fast-paced block-dropping action of Tetris with the colorful blob-matching madness of Puyo Puyo. Play various game modes, including a unique Fusion mode that blends both games' mechanics for a thrilling challenge. Whether you're a Tetris pro or a Puyo Puyo enthusiast, this game offers endless fun and competition. Dive into this delightful puzzle world on Steam.

Tetris 99

Experience Tetris like never before in this intense battle royale game. Tetris 99 pits you against 98 other players in a race to be the last one standing. Clear lines and send garbage blocks to your opponents to disrupt their gameplay. With fast-paced action and strategic gameplay, Tetris 99 is a thrilling multiplayer experience. Compete for victory and become the ultimate Tetris champion.

Tetr.io offers a modern Tetris experience with smooth gameplay and a thriving online community. Dive into competitive Tetris battles and test your skills against players from around the world. Customize your gameplay with various modes and options, and climb the global leaderboards. Whether you're a casual player or a competitive enthusiast, Tetr.io provides a fantastic Tetris experience.

Sandtrix

Sandtrix offers a unique twist on the classic Tetris gameplay by introducing sand physics. Arrange falling blocks made of sand to create stable structures while dealing with the challenging physics of sand. This creative take on Tetris provides a fresh and engaging puzzle-solving experience. Explore the world of Sandtrix and experiment with sand physics.
